Welcome to Cuse Cooks!

A blog about your college kitchen.

Whether you live on- or off-campus, I’ve got you covered. This blog is all about how to live a healthy college lifestyle from eating at the dining hall to grocery shopping on a budget to Sunday meal prepping. Also check out my resource page to learn more about food insecurity in Syracuse and how you can give back to the local community.

Who is the Chef behind Cuse Cooks?

Hi, I’m Ari! I am a senior at Syracuse University studying Information Management and Technology with a concentration in Web Design and minor in Public Communications.

My biggest passion in life is food.

Over the last year and a half, I had two amazing opportunities to spend time abroad which has strengthened my love for cookie, baking, and eating while expanding my palette across various cuisines. I spent the summer between my sophomore and junior year in Tel Aviv, Israel while interning for a food touring company. This spring I spent the semester studying abroad in Florence, Italy, and had the opportunity to travel to 11 different countries. A a passionate foodie, I was determined to experience different cultures through food. Every place I visited, I sought after their most popular dish: schnitzel in Austria, paella in Spain, Bolognese in Italy… you get the idea! Bascially, I love food, and I want to share that with you!
